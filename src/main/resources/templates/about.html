About Steve

Steve has been crafting furniture for the past 15 years, and is intimately familiar with all known crafting recipes. In addition to building all of his furniture in the shop on his own custom built crafting table, Steve also personally collects all of his building supplies by hand from local sources.

Commitment to Sustainability

We here at Steve's Furniture believe in following practices that will keep our world vibrant and livable for years to come. For every piece of furniture sold Steve will personally plant one tree.
